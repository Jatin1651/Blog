The Beginning of an End


I liked the 2am version of you,
It was honest and real too.
All the problems you had,
You used to rant it out to me while on bed!
Sometimes you cried,
Cause it was the past to which you were tied!
Everything seemed to be so small,
Once you told me all!
The way we hung up the calls,
Are you sleepy? No, not at all!
Was it too easy to open up?
Now that you have given up.
I wonder who will listen to my problems,
Now that i have heard all of yours.

The Inner me


You don’t know my story,
Oh, you don’t know me I’m sorry!
You don’t know my dealings,
How I hide my emotions and my feelings!
How I struggle throughout the day,
Trying to keep my sadness away!
How I hold back my tears,
Steering away from my fears!
Sometimes I cry,
Don’t want to stop but still I try!
Cause I am supposed to be strong,
Every decision I take should be right, not wrong!
All these sleepless nights with my mind full of thoughts,
I won’t lie, its hard to smile when life and death just fought!
